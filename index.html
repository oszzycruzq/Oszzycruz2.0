Personal Growth: Evolving is something that everybody has to do; it can be done mentally, and physically, or, if you believe in God, the connection can become stronger. I've evolved so much that when I look back on something, I feel like it was a dream, in which I enter school and come out of school on the same day. I can honestly say that I have gotten taller, smarter, and wiser. At the start of my schooling, I had bad grades, no friends, and I couldn’t even read right. Therefore, I would suffer and struggle with many things at first because of my dyslexia and the way I looked. I spent the first couple of years working on myself; for example, I worked with my dad so I could have some money for myself, and with that money, I invested in my own book, a gym membership, and even decided to learn how to swim. A smart bunny once said, "Slowly but surely, you win the race." I took my time, and every year I got better grades, got fitter, and felt my confidence rise.
